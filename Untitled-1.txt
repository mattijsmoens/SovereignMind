## An fMRI dataset for testing semantic language decoders This dataset contains data used in the paper "Semantic reconstruction of continuous language from non-invasive brain recordings" by Jerry Tang, Amanda LeBel, Shailee Jain, and Alexander G. Huth. Tang, J., LeBel, A., Jain, S. et al. Semantic reconstruction of continuous language from non-invasive brain recordings. Nat Neurosci (2023). [https://doi.org/10.1038/s41593-023-01304-9](https://doi.org/10.1038/s41593-023-01304-9) ## Dataset Derivatives 1. preprocessed data: fully preprocessed data as described in previous works. 2. textgrids: aligned transcripts of the stimulus with start and end point for each word.
